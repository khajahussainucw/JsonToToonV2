What is XML Beautifier?

Need to format your XML (eXtensible Markup Language) with custom indentation? XML Beautifier gives you more control than standard formatters. Customize indentation size to match your project's coding standards, team preferences, or personal style. Perfect when you need consistent formatting across your XML files.

According to W3C XML specification, proper formatting makes XML more readable and maintainable. Our beautifier preserves all your data and structure while giving you control over indentation. Works great with config files, API responses, or any XML that needs consistent formatting.

Everything happens in your browser – your XML never leaves your computer. Beautify, download, and use your formatted XML right away.

How It Works

Common Questions

Is my XML data stored?

Nope. Everything happens right in your browser. Your XML never leaves your computer – we don't even see it.

What's the difference between Beautifier and Formatter?

Beautifier lets you customize indentation size, while Formatter uses standard formatting. Use Beautifier when you need specific indentation, Formatter for standard formatting.

Does it change my XML structure?

No. We only add whitespace and formatting. All your data, attributes, and structure stay exactly the same. Your XML stays valid.

What indentation sizes are supported?

You can choose from 1 to 8 spaces. Most teams use 2 or 4 spaces. Pick what matches your project's style guide.

Does it work with large XML files?

Yep. Since everything happens in your browser, it depends on your device. Small to medium XML files format instantly. Really huge files might take a few seconds.

Can I use tabs instead of spaces?

Currently we support spaces only. This ensures consistent formatting across different editors and systems. Most XML style guides recommend spaces anyway.
